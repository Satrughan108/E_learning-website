Artificial Intelligence

Attention Is All You Need

Authors: Ashish Vaswani, Noam Shazeer, Niki Parmar, Jakob Uszkoreit, Llion Jones, Aidan N. Gomez, Lukasz Kaiser, Illia Polosukhin

Description: This paper introduces the Transformer model, which relies entirely on self-attention mechanisms to draw global dependencies between input and output.

ImageNet Classification with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks

Authors: Alex Krizhevsky, Ilya Sutskever, Geoffrey E. Hinton

Description: This paper demonstrates the effectiveness of deep learning through the application of deep convolutional neural networks to the ImageNet dataset.

Quantum Computing

Quantum Computation and Quantum Information

Authors: Michael A. Nielsen, Isaac L. Chuang

Description: This is a foundational textbook in the field of quantum computing, providing comprehensive coverage of the subject.

Superconducting Qubits: A Short Review

Authors: M.H. Devoret, R.J. Schoelkopf

Description: This review covers the basics of superconducting qubits, a leading physical implementation for quantum computation.

Biotechnology

CRISPR-Cas9 and Targeted Genome Editing: A New Era in Molecular Biology

Authors: Jennifer A. Doudna, Emmanuelle Charpentier

Description: This paper discusses the development and applications of CRISPR-Cas9 technology for genome editing.

The Hallmarks of Cancer

Authors: Douglas Hanahan, Robert A. Weinberg

Description: This seminal paper identifies the underlying principles that govern the transformation of normal cells into cancer cells.

Space Exploration

The Large Scale Structure of Space-Time

Authors: S.W. Hawking, G.F.R. Ellis

Description: This book provides an in-depth look at the theoretical underpinnings of our understanding of space-time.

On the Electrodynamics of Moving Bodies

Author: Albert Einstein

Description: This groundbreaking paper introduces the theory of special relativity.
